After onboarding, HQ opens the workspace setup wizard. This is where you personalize your workspace — it takes about two minutes.

## What the wizard configures

<Steps>
  <Step title="Workspace identity">
    Set your workspace name, slug, and owner profile (name, preferred name, timezone).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pipeline stages">
    Define the stages contacts move through — e.g. Lead → Qualified → Proposal → Won. These power the CRM kanban view.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Custom fields">
    Add fields to contacts and organizations beyond the defaults — company size, industry, referral source, etc.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Task streams">
    Create streams for grouping work — e.g. Content, Outreach, Product, Ops. Tasks belong to streams.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Presets

Don't want to configure from scratch? Pick a preset that seeds sensible defaults for your use case:

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Outreach" icon="paper-plane">Pipeline, fields, and streams for outbound sales or BD.</Card>
  <Card title="Job search" icon="briefcase">Track applications, contacts, and follow-ups.</Card>
  <Card title="Recruiting" icon="users">Candidate pipeline with interview stages.</Card>
  <Card title="Sales" icon="chart-line">Full sales pipeline with deal tracking.</Card>
  <Card title="Client work" icon="handshake">Project-oriented streams and client fields.</Card>
  <Card title="Blank" icon="file">Start empty and build your own.</Card>
</CardGroup>

<Tip>
  Everything the wizard configures can be changed later from Settings → Pipeline, Settings → Fields, and Settings → Streams.
</Tip>
