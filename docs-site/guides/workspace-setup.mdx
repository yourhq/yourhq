After connecting Supabase and signing in, HQ opens the **Workspace Setup** wizard. This takes about 2 minutes and sets up the core structure of your workspace.

<Info>
  Everything you configure here can be changed later from Settings → Pipeline, Settings → Fields, and Settings → Streams. Nothing is permanent. On [HQ hosted](/hosted/getting-started), the wizard runs during signup — your workspace is pre-configured when you sign in.
</Info>

---

## What the wizard configures

<Steps>
  <Step title="Workspace identity">
    Set your **workspace name** (shown in the top nav), a URL-friendly **slug**, and your **owner profile** — your name, preferred name, and timezone. Agents use your timezone to schedule work and reference times correctly.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pipeline stages">
    Define the stages contacts move through, e.g. `Lead → Qualified → Proposal → Negotiation → Won / Lost`. These power the CRM kanban view and gate routine conditions. You can reorder, rename, or add stages from Settings → Pipeline later.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Custom fields">
    Add extra fields to contacts and organizations beyond the defaults (name, email, phone, company). Examples: `Company size`, `Industry`, `Referral source`, `ICP score`. Field types: text, number, date, select, multi-select.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Task streams">
    Create streams for grouping tasks — e.g. `Content`, `Outreach`, `Product`, `Ops`. Every task belongs to a stream. Agents also use streams to organize their work.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Presets

Presets seed sensible defaults for your pipeline stages, fields, and streams so you don't have to configure from scratch. Pick one that matches your use case — you can customize everything afterward.

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Personal ops" icon="user">
    General-purpose: task streams for Content, Outreach, Admin. Minimal pipeline. Good starting point if you're unsure.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Outreach" icon="paper-plane">
    Pipeline and fields for outbound sales or business development: Lead, Contacted, Qualified, Proposal, Closed.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Job search" icon="briefcase">
    Track job applications, contacts at target companies, and follow-up tasks. Stages: Identified, Applied, Interviewing, Offer, Rejected.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Recruiting" icon="users">
    Candidate pipeline with interview stages: Sourced, Screening, Interview, Offer, Hired.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Sales" icon="chart-line">
    Full sales pipeline with deal fields: company size, ARR, close date, deal stage.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Client work" icon="handshake">
    Project-oriented setup: client contacts, project status fields, work streams per engagement.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Blank" icon="file">
    Start empty. Build the pipeline and fields from scratch.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>
